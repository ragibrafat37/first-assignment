The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Reform is at the heart of this revolution. We must unite to build a nation that values justice, sustainability, and human dignity. Together, we can create a Bangladesh where every individual has the power to shape their own destiny. The time for change is now. Let us work together to make Bangladesh 2.0 a reality.Bangladesh 2.0 is about empowering every citizen, ensuring that no one is left behind. It is about creating a future where young people do not have to seek opportunities abroad but find them right here. It is about strengthening our democracy, fighting corruption, and fostering entrepreneurship. We must encourage social businesses that put people before profit, ensuring that wealth is distributed fairly.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
